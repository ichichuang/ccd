## M7 Result

Eligible helper count: 2 runtime-neutral JSON codec helpers.

M7 did not move compression because `lz-string` is not available to `@ccd/shared-utils` through that package's manifest and manifest/lockfile edits are forbidden in this lane. Compression extraction requires a future dependency/package-manifest approval lane or an owner-approved alternative that proves exact `lz-string` format compatibility without new dependency risk.
