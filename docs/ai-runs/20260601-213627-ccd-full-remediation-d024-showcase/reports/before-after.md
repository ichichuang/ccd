# D-024 Before / After

## Before

- `scripts/ai-architecture-guard.mjs` skipped
  `apps/web-demo/src/views/example/components/primevue-collection/**`.
- Showcase files directly imported Button, Tag, InputText, Select, and useConfirm from
  PrimeVue.
- `C-06` remained open because the showcase exception still existed.

## After

- The guard no longer has a `primevue-collection/**` path exception.
- Showcase files consume `CcdButton`, `CcdTag`, `CcdInputText`, and `CcdSelect`
  from `@ccd/vue-ui`.
- Showcase confirm flows consume `usePrimeVueConfirmService()` from
  `@ccd/vue-primevue-adapter`.
- `components.d.ts` was regenerated by `pnpm build:web-demo`; `ConfirmPopup` left the
  generated registry after the overview page switched to the adapter facade.
- `auto-imports.d.ts` was formatted and verified with no diff.
